# Example: Meeting to Execution Workflow

## Scenario

**Project:** AI Governance Research Initiative  
**Purpose:** Demonstrate how a conversation can be transformed into structured execution.

*(Fictional example for workflow demonstration.)*

---

# 1. Raw Meeting Notes

Discussion points:

- Explore current AI governance challenges.
- Identify key stakeholders and perspectives.
- Research existing frameworks.
- Consider potential outputs and communication strategy.

Open questions:

- What problem should be prioritized first?
- Who needs to be involved?
- What information is missing?

---

# 2. AI-Assisted Summary

## Key Themes

The discussion focuses on understanding AI governance from multiple perspectives and identifying areas where research and action can create meaningful impact.

## Main Considerations

- Balance between innovation and responsibility.
- Importance of diverse stakeholder input.
- Need for structured research before decision-making.

# 3. Decisions Made

| Decision | Reasoning | Owner |
|---|---|---|

| Map key stakeholders | Ensure diverse perspectives | Research Team |

# 4. Action Items
