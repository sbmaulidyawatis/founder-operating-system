# Research & Decision Brief Workflow

## Purpose

Transform complex information into structured insights that support better decisions.

## Inputs

- Research questions
- Articles and reports
- Market information
- Stakeholder perspectives
- Existing documentation

## Workflow

Research Question

↓

AI-assisted Exploration

- Gather relevant information
- Summarize key concepts
- Identify patterns and themes
- Compare different perspectives
- Surface potential questions

↓

Human Analysis

- Evaluate relevance
- Connect context
- Challenge assumptions
- Determine implications

↓

Decision Brief

- Key insights
- Options and trade-offs
- Recommendations
- Next actions

↓

Knowledge Base

- Store reusable insights
- Document decisions
- Improve future research

## AI Role

AI accelerates information synthesis and pattern recognition, while human judgment remains responsible for context, evaluation, and decisions.

## Output

- Research summary
- Decision memo
- Key questions
- Recommendations
- Documented knowledge
